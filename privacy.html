Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

LightningDeals ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it. By using our website and services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to process your order and provide support:

  • Full Name — to personalise your order and communications.
  • Email Address — to deliver subscription credentials and send order confirmations.
  • WhatsApp / Mobile Number — to deliver activation details and provide support.
  • UPI Transaction ID (UTR) — for payment verification purposes only.
  • Payment Screenshot — retained temporarily for transaction verification, then deleted.

We do not collect your UPI PIN, bank account details, or any payment credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your subscription order.
  • To send you activation credentials via email or WhatsApp.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your queries.
  • To send renewal reminders before your subscription expires (you may opt out at any time).
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of what is strictly necessary to fulfil your order.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely. We use industry-standard encryption (256-bit SSL) on all pages of our website. Order data is stored in our internal system, which is accessible only to authorised staff members involved in order processing.

Payment screenshots are used solely for verification and are not stored permanently after your order is fulfilled.

4. WhatsApp Communications

When you provide your WhatsApp number, you consent to receive order-related messages from us, including activation credentials, order status updates, and support responses. We will not add you to any promotional broadcast lists without your explicit consent.

WhatsApp messages are subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for WhatsApp's data handling.

5. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to maintain your cart session, preferences, and browsing state. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect website functionality (e.g., your cart may not save items).

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., WhatsApp, UPI apps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your order information for up to 12 months for warranty and support purposes. After this period, your personal data is deleted from our systems. If you would like your data deleted earlier, please contact us and we will comply within 7 business days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to our warranty obligations).
  • Opt out of renewal reminders and non-essential communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us via WhatsApp or the contact form on our website.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
