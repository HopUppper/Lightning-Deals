Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

⚡ Our promise: If your subscription is not activated within 30 minutes of payment confirmation, you are entitled to a full refund — no questions asked. Just message us on WhatsApp.

At LightningDeals, we want every customer to feel confident purchasing from us. Because we deal in digital subscriptions, our refund policy is designed to be fair while accounting for the nature of digital goods delivery.

1. Full Refund — Eligible Cases

You are entitled to a 100% full refund in the following situations:

  • Your subscription was not activated or delivered within 30 minutes of your payment being confirmed during business hours (or within 2 hours during peak periods).
  • The activation credentials we delivered do not work, and we are unable to provide a functional replacement within 24 hours.
  • You were charged for an order but received no confirmation from us and cannot reach our support team.
  • You accidentally placed a duplicate order for the same product within 24 hours.

2. Prorated Refund — Eligible Cases

If your subscription worked initially but stopped functioning before your validity period ended, and we are unable to replace it, you are eligible for a prorated refund covering the unused remaining days.

Example: You purchased a 3-month plan, it worked for 1 month, and we cannot replace it — you receive a refund for the 2 unused months.

3. Replacement First Policy

In most cases where a subscription stops working, we will first attempt to replace it with an equivalent or better plan. Replacements are typically delivered within 24 hours. If you prefer a refund instead of a replacement, please inform us and we will honour that request.

4. Refunds NOT Available

We are unable to issue refunds in the following situations:

  • Change of mind after a subscription has been successfully activated and delivered to your account.
  • Account bans caused by your own violation of the underlying software vendor's terms of service (e.g., sharing credentials you were given with additional parties, using the tool for prohibited purposes).
  • Incompatibility with your specific device, OS configuration, or account — please check product requirements before purchasing.
  • Delays within tolerance — delivery within the stated timeframes (30 min standard / 2 hours peak) does not qualify for refund.
  • Subscriptions that have been used for more than 50% of their validity period, unless the issue is on our end.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us via WhatsApp with the following information:

  • Your Order ID (found in your confirmation message, format: LD-XXXXXXXX)
  • Your UPI Transaction ID (UTR number)
  • Reason for refund request
  • Screenshot of the issue (if applicable)

We aim to process all refund requests within 24–48 hours. Approved refunds are returned to your original UPI ID within 3–5 business days, depending on your bank's processing time.

6. Contact for Refunds

  • WhatsApp: +91 76959 56938 — usually replies within 3 minutes
  • Website: lightningdeals.online

7. Dispute Resolution

If you feel your refund request has been unfairly denied, please escalate via WhatsApp requesting a senior review. We are committed to resolving all disputes fairly and transparently. Our goal is a 100% customer satisfaction rate on legitimate refund requests.
